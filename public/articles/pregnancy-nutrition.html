Essential Nutrients During Pregnancy

Proper nutrition during pregnancy is crucial for both mother and baby's health. Here's what you need to know about essential nutrients and how to get them.

Folic Acid

  • Prevents neural tube defects
  • Found in leafy greens
  • Daily requirement: 400-800 mcg

Iron

  • Prevents anemia
  • Found in lean meats
  • Daily requirement: 27 mg

Calcium

  • Builds strong bones
  • Found in dairy products
  • Daily requirement: 1000 mg

Protein

  • Supports growth
  • Found in lean meats
  • Daily requirement: 75-100g

Foods to Include

Food Group Examples Benefits
Fruits & Vegetables Spinach, oranges, carrots Vitamins, minerals, fiber
Whole Grains Brown rice, quinoa, oats Energy, fiber, B vitamins
Lean Proteins Chicken, fish, beans Growth and development

Foods to Avoid

Foods to Limit or Avoid

  • Raw or undercooked meat and fish
  • Unpasteurized dairy products
  • High-mercury fish
  • Raw eggs
  • Alcohol and caffeine

Meal Planning Tips

Plan Ahead

Prepare healthy snacks and meals in advance

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day

Small Meals

Eat smaller, more frequent meals

When to Consult a Nutritionist

  • If you have dietary restrictions
  • If you're experiencing severe morning sickness
  • If you have gestational diabetes
  • If you're carrying multiples
