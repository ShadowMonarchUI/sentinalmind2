Yoga & Meditation

Discover the transformative power of yoga and meditation for your physical and mental well-being

Beginner 5-10 minutes

Downward-Facing Dog

A foundational pose that stretches and strengthens the entire body while calming the mind.

How to Do It:
  1. Start on your hands and knees
  2. Lift your hips up and back
  3. Straighten your legs
  4. Press your heels toward the floor
  5. Keep your head between your arms
Benefits:
  • Strengthens arms and shoulders
  • Stretches hamstrings and calves
  • Improves circulation
  • Relieves back pain
  • Calms the mind
Intermediate 30-60 seconds per side

Warrior I

A powerful standing pose that builds strength and stability while opening the chest and hips.

How to Do It:
  1. Step your right foot forward
  2. Bend your right knee to 90 degrees
  3. Square your hips forward
  4. Raise your arms overhead
  5. Keep your back leg straight
Benefits:
  • Strengthens legs and core
  • Improves balance
  • Opens chest and shoulders
  • Increases stamina
  • Builds confidence
Intermediate 30-60 seconds per side

Tree Pose

A balancing pose that improves focus and stability while strengthening the legs and core.

How to Do It:
  1. Stand on one leg
  2. Place the other foot on inner thigh
  3. Bring hands to heart center
  4. Focus on a steady point
  5. Keep your core engaged
Benefits:
  • Improves balance
  • Strengthens legs
  • Enhances focus
  • Opens hips
  • Builds stability

Meditation Techniques

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on your breath and present moment awareness.

  • Find a quiet space
  • Sit comfortably
  • Focus on your breath
  • Observe your thoughts
  • Return to breath
Loving-Kindness Meditation

Cultivate compassion and positive emotions.

  • Start with self-love
  • Extend to loved ones
  • Include neutral people
  • Extend to all beings
  • Feel the connection
Body Scan Meditation

Develop body awareness and release tension.

  • Lie down comfortably
  • Focus on each body part
  • Release tension
  • Observe sensations
  • Stay present
