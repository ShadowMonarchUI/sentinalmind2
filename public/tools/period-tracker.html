Understanding Your Cycle

Your menstrual cycle is divided into four phases, each with its own characteristics and hormonal changes.

Cycle Phases

  • Menstrual Phase (Days 1-5)
  • Follicular Phase (Days 6-14)
  • Ovulation (Day 14)
  • Luteal Phase (Days 15-28)

Important Notes

  • Track symptoms regularly
  • Note any irregularities
  • Consult a doctor if concerned
  • Keep a mood journal
