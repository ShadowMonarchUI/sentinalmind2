Breathing Exercise Guide
4-7-8 Breathing Technique
  1. Find a comfortable seated position
  2. Place your tongue tip behind your upper front teeth
  3. Exhale completely through your mouth
  4. Close your mouth and inhale through your nose for 4 counts
  5. Hold your breath for 7 counts
  6. Exhale through your mouth for 8 counts
  7. Repeat 4 times
Meditation Guide
Mindfulness Meditation Steps
  1. Find a quiet, comfortable space
  2. Set a timer for 5-10 minutes
  3. Focus on your breath
  4. Notice thoughts without judgment
  5. Return to breath when mind wanders
  6. Practice daily for best results
Gentle Yoga Guide
Basic Gentle Yoga Sequence
  1. Cat-Cow Stretch (5 breaths)
  2. Child's Pose (1 minute)
  3. Mountain Pose (5 breaths)
  4. Gentle Forward Fold (5 breaths)
  5. Easy Seated Twist (each side)
  6. Savasana (5 minutes)
Tai Chi Guide
Basic Tai Chi Principles
